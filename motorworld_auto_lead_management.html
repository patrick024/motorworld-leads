Overview

The Auto Lead Management Response System is a fully automated lead handling solution built for MOTORWORLD. Designed to streamline the inquiry process, this system ensures that every potential customer receives an immediate, accurate, and professional response — without any manual intervention.

When a customer submits an inquiry through the MOTORWORLD inquiry form, the system instantly detects the new response, automatically looks up pricing from a centralized database, notifies the sales team via Slack, and sends a personalized email to the customer — all within seconds.

How it works — 4-step automation
1
📋 Google Forms
Lead Capture
Customer submits their name, email, preferred vehicle model, and payment terms (Cash or Financing) via the inquiry form.
2
📊 Google Sheets
Price Lookup
The system auto-fetches the SRP, cash discount, and financing terms (12–60 months) from the centralized price list database.
3
💬 Slack
Team Alert
The sales team receives an instant notification with the customer's name, email, inquired model, and SRP in the designated channel.
4
📧 Gmail
Auto Reply
The customer receives a personalized email with full vehicle details, SRP, cash discount, and available financing terms up to 60 months.
